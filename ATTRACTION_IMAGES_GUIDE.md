## Image Requirements
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Recommended 1200x800px or larger
- **Quality**: High resolution for best display
- **Count**: 4 images per attraction (minimum)

## Where to Get Images
1. Take photos yourself when visiting these places
2. Use stock photo services with location-specific searches
3. Hire a photographer to capture these attractions
4. Use images from official tourism websites (with permission)
